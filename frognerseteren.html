FROGNERSETEREN

Korketrekkeren

Korketrekkeren is a popular sledding hill in Oslo that runs for about 2500m from Frognerseteren station to Midstuen station. The hill is usually prepared every morning in the winter season. You can rent a toboggan at the top of the hill.

Frognerseteren

Frognerseteren is a popular year-round hiking area in Nordmarka, Oslo. Frognerseteren became an even more used starting point for trips in Nordmarka both summer and winter after Holmenkollbanen was opened in 1898, and especially after it was extended to Frognerseteren station in 1916.

Tryvann

Oslo Vinterpark is a ski resort in the area of Tryvannshøgda. The resort has 18 slopes with 11 lifts. The first ski trail in the area opened in 1938 as the first north of the Alps. The longest trail of the resort is Wyllerløypa at 1400 meters with a fall of 381 meters.
