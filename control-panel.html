Fun facts about the Subway in Oslo

- The subway had the maiden voyage on May 22, 1966, where King Harald Olav V was on board.

- Ryen Station is the main workshop for the subways. This workshop will use solar cells for a greener alternative

-The trains are designed by Porsche and manufactured by Siemens
