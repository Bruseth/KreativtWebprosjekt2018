The Final Stop

The Kolsås stop is the final stop of line 3. Follow the signs to Kolsåstoppen from the station and enjoy the hike!

Kolsåstoppen

Kolsåstoppen is a popular destination for a Sunday hike. It is one of the largest mountains in the area and reaches 379moh.

The View

At the top there is a fantastic panoramic ciew over Oslo, Asker and Bærum and the Oslo fjord.

Winter Time

Want to hike to the top at winter time? The view over Oslo in winter is something you will never forget! However, walk carefully, there will be ice.
