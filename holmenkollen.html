Holmenkollen

The Holmenkollen area is a gateway to Nordmarka, one of the most popular recreational areas in the city. Tryvann and Frognerseteren are great starting points for hikes and trips summer and winter alike.

Finstua

The Restaurants in Holmenkollen are good places to try traditional Norwegian foods, be it five-course meals based on local ingredients or the famous apple cake served at Frognerseteren Restaurant. Great views of the city come standard.

Scandic Holmenkollen

The hills around Holmenkollen offers top-class hotels with conference facilities and a great view of Oslo.
